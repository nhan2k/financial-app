# Security Policy

## Reporting Vulnerabilities

If you discover a security vulnerability in this project, please report it privately to us.

**Email:** security@example.com

Please do not disclose publicly reported vulnerabilities until we have had a chance to address them.

## What to Include

When reporting a vulnerability, please include:
- A description of the issue
- Steps to reproduce the vulnerability
- Any potential impact you've identified
- If possible, a suggested fix or mitigation

## Response Timeline

We aim to acknowledge receipt of security reports within a few days and will keep you informed of our progress toward a fix or mitigation.

## Disclosure Policy

We believe in responsible disclosure. Once a vulnerability is fixed, we will announce it publicly and credit the reporter (unless you prefer to remain anonymous).
